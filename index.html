About Event

After the successful 1st Conference - "Agile Carnival Chandigarh 2015" we are back with our Annual event @ ISB Mohali Campus on 7th-8th May.This time it is going to be a workshop + case study based conference and less of "talking". Professionals who wanted to learn more about Product Development Framework like Kanban SAFe etc should definitely join this conference and learn from the industry experts.
